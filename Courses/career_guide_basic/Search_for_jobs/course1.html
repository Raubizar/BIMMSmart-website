1. Define Your Target Job

Identify one job title, one industry, and one location that best align with your career goals.

2. Use Boolean Search for Precision

  • AND: Ensures both terms appear (e.g., "BIM Manager" AND "Data Center").
  • OR: Expands results (e.g., "BIM Manager" OR "Digital Construction").
  • NOT: Excludes terms (e.g., "BIM Coordinator" NOT "Intern").
  • "Quotes": Searches exact phrases (e.g., "ISO 19650").
  • *: Wildcard for variations (e.g., "BIM Coord*").
  • Parentheses: Groups terms (e.g., ("BIM Manager" OR "BIM Lead") AND ("Ireland" OR "UK")).
